    As of August 12th, there have been:


    257 mass shootings1 in 2019. August 12th is 224th Day of the Year, meaning there have been more shootings than days in the year.2

    34,306 gun incidents in 2019.2

    9,083 lives taken.2

    18,015 injuries2 as a result of gun violence.

    100 Americans die by gun violence every day, with hundreds more being shot and injured.3


    Every year, nearly 1,700 children / teens die by gun homicide. For those under 13, domestic or family violence are most frequently the cause of gun homocides.3


    As of April 4, 2019, approximately three million American children are witnesses of gun violence every year.3
