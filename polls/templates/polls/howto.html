How to Prepare

    Mass shootings are all too common in the United States. Use this guide in case you are stuck in a situation in which an active shooter is in your presence.


    Tip: Always be aware of emergency exits around you

RUN

Away from the shooter. Try and run in a zigzagging pattern to reduce the chance of being shot if targeted at and try and blend into the crowd if there is one.

Don't think

Remember, your life is in danger. Don't take time to gather your stuff or pause for a second to get your thoughts together. Get out of there!

    Try and save others

    While trying to escape, bring others along that do not know what to do or are not attempting to escape. You may save a life, and traveling to safety with a group is infinetely safer than going alone.

    If you can not run: hide

    Hide out of side, and preferably in a place that is lockable and able to barricade. Remain completely quiet until confirmed safe.

Last resort: Attack

If the shooter has burst into the room you are in, don't just stand there and wait to get shot. Do anything you can to hurt the shooter, like jumping on top of them, preferably when they are reloading. The more people that attack the shooter, the more of a chance you have of defeating them. This is only as a last resort though, if your life is in jeparody.
